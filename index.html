Specialized Projects Showcase

Thermal Design / Automation

E-COLD HDW 240 - Water-cooled chiller

Chiller for high capacities, E-COLD family line developed while working for Frigocenter. Equipped with 2 trios (3-compressor layout), brazed plate heat exchanger, hydraulic pumps skid, and shell-and-tube condenser. This model specifically will operate for air-conditioned areas of an industry. Its structure was developed with laser-cut sheet metal and electrostatic painting. It uses Nuklwo FS software, which was developed by the Automation team at Frigocenter, allowing users to easily control the equipment's parameters and set-point temperature remotely.
Industrial Refrigeration

Blast-chilling static tunnel

This model is used at the end of production lines to quickly freeze food to later be stored. Commonly used for meat and bread. Manufactured configurations include modular scaling setups: 4 food carts, 7 food carts, and 10 food carts capacity profiles.
